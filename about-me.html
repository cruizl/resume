About Me

I am Carlos Ruiz, a 26 year old--currently amateur-- web developer from Ecuador.

Funny enough, I went to medical school after high school and actually graduated as a Medical Doctor. Later on, after some difficult moments I decided I wanted to switch careers and move into a passion that I always had but never explored--web development

During high school, I ran an indie tumblr blog about photography and clothes that I liked at the time. For some reason, the default themes Tumblr would offer felt like they lacked something, maybe a bit of my own touch. Eventually I started to find "code" for other themes I found over the internet and the time it all looked like gibberish to me. But that did not stop me.

I started to delete stuff, move things around, see how the whole blog would be affected if I typed a couple words with weird symbols and I discovered I actually enjoyed getting stuff wrong and then trying again and getting them right. Playing with random keyboard strokes would add beauty and style to a collection of posts I already found absolutely pleasing to look at, and that felt glorious. Just thinking of doing stuff like that from a scratch, seemed impossible.

After medical school, I took some courses and realized that maybe it was not that difficult. So here I am, making a portfolio of the work that I made with so much love for you all to see. I hope you enjoye it.

Thanks for taking the time to read about my story!
